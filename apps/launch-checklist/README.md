# Launch Checklist

Run product launches from your phone without missing critical steps.

Generated by Android Autopilot.

## Target user
Indie founders and small product teams shipping launches, campaigns, and releases.

## Problem
Launch tasks get scattered across chats and docs, causing missed steps, late checks, and chaotic release day coordination.

## Solution
A mobile launch control room with structured pre-launch, launch-day, and post-launch checklists plus owners and status snapshots.

## V1 features
- Launch templates for product release, campaign, and feature rollout
- Owner and deadline fields for each checklist item
- Critical blocker section with severity tags
- Today view for tasks due now
- Launch notes and retro summary
- Offline-first sample data mode

## Monetization
Free for one launch board, then Pro for unlimited boards and templates.
